1. Wait for all pods to be Running:
   kubectl get pods -l app.kubernetes.io/instance={{ .Release.Name }} -w

2. Get the external LoadBalancer address:
   kubectl get svc {{ .Release.Name }}-loadbalancer

3. Once the ADDRESS is assigned, upgrade with the real public URLs:
   helm upgrade {{ .Release.Name }} . \
     --set frontend.publicBackendUrl="http://<ADDRESS>:3000/api/proxy" \
     --set frontend.publicWsUrl="ws://<ADDRESS>:3001" \
     --set secrets.redisUrl="redis://<your-redis>:6379"

4. Verify WebSocket pods are on different nodes (podAntiAffinity):
   kubectl get pods -l app={{ .Release.Name }}-ws -o wide

5. Health check:
   kubectl port-forward svc/{{ .Release.Name }}-ws 1234
   curl http://localhost:1234/health
